# Project Brief: Media & Comms Corp Operations Dashboard

## What We're Building
A real-time operational dashboard for content strategists and audience insights managers at media & communications companies. Users will monitor subscriber health, analyze content performance across formats, track ad revenue impact, and identify growth opportunities — all in one unified interface.

## Why It Matters
Content teams need a single source of truth to answer critical questions: *Is my content working? What's driving subscriptions? Why are people leaving? Where's the ad revenue coming from?* Today, this data is scattered across multiple tools. Our dashboard brings it together with sophisticated interactive analytics.

## Core Audience
- **Content Strategists:** Decide what content to create and prioritize based on performance
- **Audience Insights Managers:** Analyze trends, report findings, quantify content impact

## Key Features (MVP)
1. **Subscriber Health** — Track growth, churn rates, cohort retention, lifetime value
2. **Content Performance** — Views, engagement rates, format breakdown, new subscriptions attributed
3. **Revenue Tracking** — Ad revenue trends, revenue per format, RPM (revenue per 1k views)
4. **Correlation Analysis** — See which content drives subscriptions and revenue
5. **Real-Time Updates** — Dashboard refreshes every 30-60 seconds
6. **Flexible Filtering** — View data across 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or custom ranges
7. **Export & Sharing** — Generate CSV/PDF reports and share dashboard links
8. **Full Mobile Support** — Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and phone

## Success = 
- Dashboard loads in <2 seconds
- Users can answer a performance question in <3 clicks
- Export a report in <5 seconds
- Works across all devices with sophisticated charts and interactions

## Technology
Next.js 14 + React 18 for the frontend, Material-UI for components, Recharts + D3 for sophisticated charts, Zustand for state, mock data for MVP, Vercel for hosting.

## Design Philosophy
Bold, creative, premium look with dark theme. Cyan/blue palette conveys trust and growth. Data is the hero — UI stays out of the way. Professional yet approachable.
